About Me

I am a front-end developer specialized in transforming design mockups into clean, responsive, and fully functional websites using HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, and JavaScript.

My main specialty is converting visual concepts into real websites that faithfully respect the original design while delivering strong performance in real-world conditions.

I pay close attention to details that are often overlooked during development: spacing consistency, responsive behavior, visual hierarchy, clean layout structure, readable code structure, and smooth interactions.

My approach is different: I focus on lightweight, organized, and efficient solutions to keep websites fast, performant, and sustainable.

Beyond front-end development, I have a strong interest in SEO and web visibility. I don't see a website as just a visual product, but as a tool designed to be found, used, and to inspire trust.

I value clear communication, reliability, and respect for deadlines. My goal is simple: understand the need, build with precision, and deliver solid results.
